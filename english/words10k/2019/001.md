# 1/10000 - [waive](https://www.ldoceonline.com/dictionary/waive)

* waive [weiv] v[T]
  * to state officially that a right, rule etc can be ignored
  * 宣布放弃(权利)；宣布免除(规则)
  * e.g. She waived her right to see a lawyer. 她放弃了见律师的权利。
